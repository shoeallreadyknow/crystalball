        1. What is Etoile?
        - Etoile means The star of the destiny in French. It is a fortune-telling application which is capable of checking your fortune for today and tomorrow according to basic personal information you input. And it also can balance your fortune with somebody else.

        2. How to use
        - Etoile consists of todays fortune, Harmony, configuration and widget.

        2.1. The fortune of today
        - It shows the fortune of today and tomorrows according to the basic information you input. Also you can share your fortune with your friends by Twitter, Facebook or Me2day in real time.
        - In harmony section, you can check out the information of harmony between you and your opponent.
        - You can edit your basic personal information in configuration section.
        - Etoile offers the functions which can help registering your friends information so you can check your friends fortune easily and also can text the friends fortune to your buddy in Managing friends section.
        - You can briefly meet your fortune by short text and little icon everyday through activating this widget.
        (We never abuse every users personal information which you input to check your fortune-telling in any circumstances.)

        3. Etoile Tip
        - Hope you enjoy the aspects of little different data of the harmony between me to you and you to me.
